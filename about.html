All about hacking

Hacking is the unauthorized access and manipulation of computer systems, networks, websites, or mobile devices. Hackers use a variety of techniques to gain access to a system, including:
Phishing A social engineering technique that involves creating fake emails, websites, or social media profiles to trick people into revealing sensitive information or installing malware Malware
The use of viruses, worms, adware, or ransomware to gain access to a system Social engineering
Beginners should start with the fundamental cybersecurity skills one will always need when hacking: Networking, Linux, Windows, and scripting. Regardless of how advanced or basic your exploits become, you'll always call upon the knowledge and skills related to these core domains.

Regardless of how advanced or basic your exploits become, you'll always call upon the knowledge and skills related to these core domains.There are many types of hackers, including: Black hat hackers Also known as cyber criminals, they are motivated by financial gain and exploit vulnerabilities to steal data, install malware, or disrupt operations. White hat hackers Also known as ethical hackers, they are cybersecurity professionals who work with organizations to identify and fix security vulnerabilities. Gray hat hackers They are somewhere in the middle, and may inform companies that they've been hacked but ask for a fee for details. Red hat hackers They are vigilantes who target black hat hackers to expose their data and activities to the public. Green hat hackers They are new hackers who are still learning the basics of hacking and are eager to experiment. Blue hat hackers They are external security professionals that companies bring in to test their systems and networks for security weaknesses. Purple hat hackers They hack their own systems or networks to learn and upskill themselves. Whistleblowers They work from inside organizations to expose illegal, abusive, or immoral activity. Malicious insiders They work from inside organizations to pursue personal vendettas. The terms "black hat", "white hat", and "gray hat" are borrowed from old westerns, where the good guys wore white hats and the villains wore black hats.
